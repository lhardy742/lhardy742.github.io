About Me

Instructional Assistant
UCSD CSE Department
September 2023 - Present
CSE 11 - Introduction to CS
Tutoring this course, I had the pleasure of introducing students to their first programming course, supporting students in their learning process as they discovered what Computer Science looks like in a college environment. Tutoring this class was hands-down one of the most rewarding expriiences I have ever been able to take a part of. Watching and helping guide students to their own understanding of foundational concepts was very fulfilling for me, and having everybody I'm helping be peers made my work feel less like a job and more like I was helping out a friend.
CSE 12 - Data Structures
For my second quarter, CSE 12 let me continue tutoring the same students, fostering a sense of community with continuing students. For this course I was able to join the development team for the Programming Assignments, meeting with a small group to brainstorm ideas and then developing assignments for our students that best helped their learning. Writing code that was vital to 400+ students was a highly valuable learning expirience.
Android Development, Agile Methodologies
CSE 110 - Software Engineering
In this course, I worked within a group of 7 students to develop and design an Android Application, employing principles discussed in class such as the
  • Single Responsibility Principle
  • Open Closed Principle
  • Iterative Development
  • Dependency Inversion
  • Design patterns: Strategy, Observer, MVP, Factory & Builder
Our application leveraged persistent databases to retain a user's task list, offering the functionality to add tasks for today, tomorrow, and set up recurring reminders for tasks requiring regular attention.
